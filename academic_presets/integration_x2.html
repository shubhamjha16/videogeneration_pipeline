Concept Explanation

We evaluate the definite integral of x squared from 4 to 9, written as the integral from 4 to 9 of x squared dx. A definite integral gives the net area under the curve between two limits. The graph of y equals x squared is a parabola that opens upward. The shaded region under the parabola from x equals 4 to x equals 9 represents the integral value.

Graph Visualization

Plot the parabola y equals x squared on the coordinate axes. Mark the vertical lines at x equals 4 and x equals 9. The shaded area between these vertical lines and under the curve gives the integral value of 665 divided by 3.

Solution

Step 1: Apply the power rule formula

The antiderivative formula for x to the power n is x to the power n plus 1 divided by n plus 1. For x squared where n equals 2, the antiderivative is x cubed divided by 3.

Step 2: Set up the definite integral

Therefore, the integral from 4 to 9 of x squared dx equals x cubed divided by 3, evaluated from 4 to 9. We apply the fundamental theorem of calculus: subtract the lower limit value from the upper limit value.

Step 3: Calculate the upper limit

Substituting x equals 9: 9 cubed equals 729. Then 729 divided by 3 equals 243.

Step 4: Calculate the lower limit

Substituting x equals 4: 4 cubed equals 64. Then 64 divided by 3 equals 21.33.

Step 5: Subtract to get the result

Final answer equals 243 minus 64 divided by 3 equals 729 divided by 3 minus 64 divided by 3 equals 665 divided by 3 which is approximately 221.67.

Formula Used

Power rule: integral of x to the n equals x to the n plus 1 divided by n plus 1. Definite integral result: 665 divided by 3, approximately 221.67.

Key Points

  • Power rule: the antiderivative of x squared is x cubed over 3
  • Upper bound calculation: 9 cubed divided by 3 equals 243
  • Lower bound calculation: 4 cubed divided by 3 equals 64 over 3
  • Final result: 665 over 3, approximately 221.67
  • Geometrically this equals the shaded area under the parabola from 4 to 9
