To find the integral of natural log x, we use the method of integration by parts. We choose 'u' as ln x and 'dv' as dx. Differentiating 'u' gives us 'du' equals 1 over x dx, and integrating 'dv' gives us 'v' equals x. Applying the parts formula, which is integral of u dv equals uv minus integral of v du, we get x ln x minus the integral of x times 1 over x dx. The second term simplifies to the integral of 1, which is just x. Thus, the final answer is x ln x minus x plus c.
