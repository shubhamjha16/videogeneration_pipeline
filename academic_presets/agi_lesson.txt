When will Artificial General Intelligence, or AGI, finally arrive? Experts differ wildly on the timeline, but recent breakthroughs suggest it could be sooner than we think. Many top researchers predict AGI could emerge by 2030, driven by the exponential scaling of large language models. However, skeptics argue that we still lack fundamental innovations in reasoning, reliability, and long-term memory. Regardless of the exact year it happens, the race towards superintelligence is accelerating faster than ever before.
