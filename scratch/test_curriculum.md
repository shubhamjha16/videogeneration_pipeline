# Pulmonary Edema Management

Pulmonary edema is a medical emergency requiring immediate attention. The goals of treatment are to improve oxygenation and reduce pulmonary congestion.

### Key Points
- **Oxygen Therapy**: Supplemental oxygen is essential to maintain saturation.
- **Diuretics**: Furosemide helps reduce fluid volume.
- **Nitrates**: Vasodilators reduce preload and afterload.

### MCQ Selection
Which medication is first-line for reducing preload in acute cardiogenic pulmonary edema?
A. Furosemide
B. Digoxin
C. Atropine
D. Amiodarone

**Correct Answer: A**
Furosemide acts as both a diuretic and a rapid venodilator, reducing preload immediately.
